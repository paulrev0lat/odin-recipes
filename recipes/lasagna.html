The Lasagna recipe

Description

Lasagne is a classic Italian al forno dish that generally consists of alternating layers of pasta sheets and various fillings.

Including - though not exclusively - ragù, besciamella and cheese, such as mozzarella, ricotta or Parmesan.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Make meat sauce
  2. Chop the beef
  3. Pour the lasagna into the sauce
  4. Heat the oven to 180°C
  5. Bake for 45 minutes
