The Onion recipe

Description

Onion soup is a classic French dish that consists of an onion soup covered with cheese.

It is a very popular meal in classic French restaurants.

Ingredients

  • Onions
  • Emmental cheese
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Cut onions
  2. Boil water
  3. Cook the onion for 1 hour
  4. Put the cheese on it
  5. Bake it for 15 minutes in the hoven
